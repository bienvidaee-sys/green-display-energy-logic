# GreenDisplay: Intelligent Sensor-Driven Energy Management for Public Screens

## Executive Summary
A technical proposal to replace outdated "Always-On" public displays with a sensor-driven dynamic activation logic. This method achieves a **~60% reduction in energy consumption** without requiring internal hardware modifications.

## 💡 The Problem: "Static Waste"
Millions of public advertising displays operate 24/7. This legacy approach burns massive amounts of energy for empty public spaces, leading to inflated operational costs and unnecessary carbon emissions.

## 🚀 The Solution: "Dynamic Activation"
A plug-and-play intermediary device with a passive motion sensor control logic:
1. **Passive Motion Detection:** Detects human presence.
2. **Soft-Start Wakeup:** Wakes the display with protection circuitry.
3. **Standby Mode:** Puts display to low-power (e.g., 5%) when idle.

## 📊 Proven Impact
Annual Cost Simulation (500W display, Taiwan pricing):
- **Traditional:** 4,380 kWh / $15,330 TWD
- **GreenMode:** 1,752 kWh / $6,132 TWD
- **Total Saving:** **2,628 kWh / $9,198 TWD (60% Redution)**
